STAGE 2

Goal: Maintain your new weight and live a healthy lifestyle by eating well, exercising regularly, and staying active.

How To Do It

- Take FENIX once a day
- Eat a healthy meal for your second and third meals
- Supplement your three meals with two pro meals per day (healthy snacks)

What You Need To Do

- Complete the FENIX Challenge

- Head to X4Ever.club once a month and share your story about one of the following each month:
- How do you live X4ever?
- How do you maintain X4ever?
- How do you eat X4ever?
- How do you exercise X4ever?
